Computer Science & Engineering

Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 Years Intake: 60 Students

The Mechanical Engineering program focuses on design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. Our labs are equipped with modern machinery and software for practical learning.

Civil Engineering

Duration: 4 Years Intake: 60 Students
